Service Level Objectives (SLO)

Overview

Our Service Level Objectives (SLO) define the level of service our users can expect. We are committed to maintaining high performance and reliability across all services we offer.

Key Metrics

Metric Target Current Performance
Uptime 99.9% 99.95%
Response Time Under 2 seconds 1.5 seconds
Transaction Success Rate 99.5% 99.7%
Data Consistency 99.99% 99.98%

Incident Management

We follow a strict incident management protocol to ensure that any service interruptions are dealt with promptly and efficiently. We aim to restore services within 1 hour for critical issues.

Reporting

We will provide monthly performance reports to track our progress towards meeting our SLOs and to identify areas for improvement.
